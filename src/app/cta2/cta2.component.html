#2 RIDE MORE. DRIVE LESS.

Around 68% of our journeys are under 5 miles. 1 in 2 of these are in a car. Yet the average person takes only 20 minutes to cycle 5 miles.

If each of our short journeys were on a bike, not a car, each one of us could save over 200 kilos of CO2 per year.

Across the UK, that would be 24 billion journeys and 14 million tonnes of CO2 per year.

That’s equivalent to 4% of the UK’s total carbon emissions. And 4% closer to net zero.
It doesn’t have to be every day. Once a week, once a month - it makes a huge difference.

EVERY MILE COUNTS.
