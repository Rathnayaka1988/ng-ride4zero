#2 RIDE MORE. DRIVE LESS.

Around 68% of all vehicle journeys are less than 5 miles in distance, which would take the average person less than 20 minutes to ride.

If each of these journeys were by ridden by bike, each person would save over 360kg of C02 per year and we would have reduced overall carbon emissions by a whopping X%.

Not everyone can easily reduce the amount that they drive, but even riding once a week - or even once a month - instead of driving can make a huge difference.

Remember, EVERY MILE COUNTS.
